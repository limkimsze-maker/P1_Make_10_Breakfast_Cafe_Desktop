Game Info
Level 1 — shape recognition
Level 2 — customer describes a cake shape and the baker chooses the correct cake
Level 3 — look at 2 cake trays and decide how the cakes are grouped
Level 4 — count whole, half, and quarter cakes in cake figures
Level 5 — find the 2 cake shapes in a composite cake
Level 6 — bonus round with 1 challenge from each level; main score stays from Levels 1 to 5; get all 5 correct to unlock the secret 5 tiered cake
